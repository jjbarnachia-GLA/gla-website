How It Works

Three seconds.
Total clarity.

No gadgets. No guesswork. Just point your camera and get shot-shaping data that used to require a launch monitor.

Step 01
📷
Aim at the ball

Open the app and frame your ball in the lie. No special setup — just your phone camera pointing at the ground.

Step 02
🧠

🎯

Receive a clear club recommendation with expected launch variance, spin change, and ball flight adjustment.

Vision

GLA uses a MobileNetV3Small architecture — a lean, high-efficiency 13-class TFLite model trained on a proprietary dataset of complex lie interactions captured across real course conditions. It classifies everything from tight fairway to gnarly rough to bunker faces, optimized for speed and accuracy on every shot.
