Operational
Field Guide

Standard operating procedures for deploying the GLA Neural Interface in live turf environments. Follow sequence for optimal data accuracy.

01

Initialize Sequence

Launch the application. Ensure camera permissions are granted. The neural engine will require 2-3 seconds to load local weights into RAM.

Status: Ready State
02

Acquire Target

Position the golf ball within the central reticle. Hold device steady between 18-24 inches from the turf. The glowing bounding box indicates successful object detection.

Action: Hold Steady
03

Interpret Telemetry

Once analyzed, data outputs will appear on the HUD. Review "Lie Severity" (percentage buried) and "Spin Decay" predictions before executing shot.

Output: Data Ready

Mission Optimizers

Lighting Conditions

Model performs best in daylight or overcast conditions. Avoid harsh shadows directly across the ball if possible.

Device Stability

Excessive movement during the 0.2s inference scan may reduce confidence interval. Hold briefly after alignment.
